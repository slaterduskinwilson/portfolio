Spotify, But It Doesn't Make Me Angry

A Spotify clone that doesn't try to influence what you listen to.

  • Font end: React, React Bootstrap
  • Back end: Node.js, Express, The Spotify Developer API, Spotify Web API Node
  • Full search, playback, and playlist editing functionality
  • Displays song lyrics during playback
  • Doesn't break your concentration by advertising music it thinks you'll like based on data it took from you, didn't pay you for, and plans to sell to other people you don't know.
  • If you listen to 10 seconds of a particular podcast, it won't forever more declare that it's one of YOUR SHOWS and advertise it to you on your home screen every day. HEY MATT YOU LUV JOE ROGAN DONT U LOOK THERES A NEW EPISODE LOOK LOOK MATT HEY MATT
  • Authenticate with your Spotify account. Unfortunately, you do need a premium account to use it.
